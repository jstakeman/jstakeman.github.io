Domain Attack

I was very excited to launch back into writing this morning, but when I went to the site there was only an error. My browser couldn't find incrayon.com DNS record.

I checked the hosting to see if they were doing all right and Github reported no problems.

I remembered that I registered my domain through namecheap.com and a quick google search revealed this update

DNS servers under attack!

I'd never thought of that aspect of picking my registrar and DNS provider. Does anyone keep a record of how often and how successfully they get attacked? Web hosts often tout u their uptime, but I'd never thought to look for that with DNS services.

Anyone have any recommendations or strategies they use when looking at this sort of stuff?
