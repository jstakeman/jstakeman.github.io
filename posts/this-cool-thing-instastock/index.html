This cool thing: Instastock

Sometimes staring blankly into the void of the internet produces interesting results. It's like fishing. There are days when you sit contemplatively looking out on the placid waters (drunk), and there are days when you reel in something worthwhile.

Today was the latter.

Instastock is a relatively new project from Dylan Simel.

He joins the growing number of people and sites giving royalty free stock photos away… for free.

Unsplash and other sites have been invaluable to me in my day job, where I try to make a lot of online courses less ugly.

Dylan's work though interests me on a number of levels.

  • I admire his discipline of adding an image a day.
  • The glimpses of North Carolina and his travels offer valuable and different perspectives.
  • It is all shot with his iPhone.

After learning about his site, I immediately sat up from my internet fishing and

  • decided that I need that discipline of doing something every day
  • realized that my unique contexts and circumstances might offer valuable and different perspectives
  • remembered that I hate taking pictures with my Galaxy S4

Thanks for that Dylan Simel… thanks
